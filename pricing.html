Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is GEO?

Generative Engine Optimization is the practice of structuring your online presence so AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity can find and cite your business. It's like SEO, but for AI search instead of Google.

How is this different from SEO?

SEO optimises for Google's ranking algorithm. GEO optimises for how AI tools read, understand, and cite your content. Being number one on Google doesn't mean ChatGPT will mention you. They're completely different systems with different rules.

How quickly will I see results?

It depends on the AI tool. Perplexity searches the web in real time, so press release mentions can appear within days — that's usually your first visible win. Google AI Overviews are also relatively fast since they pull from Google's live index. ChatGPT and Claude update their training data periodically, so those typically take 2 to 8 weeks. We recommend checking Perplexity first for early proof that it's working.

How do I know it's working?

The simplest test: type a relevant query into ChatGPT and see if your business comes up. We also provide monthly citation tracking reports that show exactly where and how often AI tools mention you.

What about press releases — how does that help with AI?

Press releases get published on news sites that AI tools actively crawl. A well structured press release puts your brand name, location, and services into multiple high authority sources that AI models draw from when generating answers. It's one of the most effective GEO tactics available right now.

Is there a contract on the monthly plan?

No long term contract. You can cancel the monthly plan at any time. We recommend at least three months to see the full impact, but you're never locked in.
