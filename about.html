About FastGEO

The UK's first agency dedicated to getting businesses found by AI search tools.

The Short Version

More people are asking ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity for recommendations instead of scrolling through Google. If your business doesn't show up in those answers, you're invisible to a growing chunk of your market.

FastGEO fixes that. We optimise your online presence so AI tools find you, trust you, and recommend you to the people searching for exactly what you do.

Why This Exists

I'm Dan Moughtin. I've spent years working with small businesses on their digital presence, and I kept seeing the same problem: business owners who'd invested in websites, paid for SEO, built up Google reviews, but were completely absent from AI search results.

The thing is, AI tools don't work like Google. They don't just match keywords. They look for structured information, third party signals, consistent details across the web, and content that actually answers the question someone asked.

Most businesses have none of that set up properly. Not because they've done anything wrong, but because nobody told them they needed to. That's what FastGEO is here to change.

How We Work

We start with a free scan that scores your website across SEO and GEO signals. You get a score out of 100, a quick win you can action straight away, and a clear picture of where you stand.

From there, our paid packages handle everything: on page optimisation, schema markup, press releases that get picked up by AI tools, review strategy, citation building, and ongoing monitoring to make sure you stay visible as AI search evolves.

No jargon, no 12 month contracts on the monthly plans, no vague promises. Just measurable improvements to how often AI tools mention your business.
